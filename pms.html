Dietary Changes

Add calcium-rich foods, like yogurt and leafy green vegetables to your diet. Avoid caffeine.
Eat six small meals a day rather than three large ones,
or eat slightly less at your three meals and add three light snacks.

Dr.Preeti Patel

Do Relaxation Methods

Relaxation therapy may include breathing exercises, meditation, and yoga.
Massage therapy is another form of relaxation therapy that you may want to try.

Dr.Vidhi Panchani

Magnesium supplements

Magnesium deficiency can cause anxiety, depression, irritability, and muscle weakness.
Taking a magnesium supplement has been suggested to help relieve from these symptoms.

Dr.Rishabh Parmar
